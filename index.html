    Captivating Stories, Exceptionally Told

    With a passion for storytelling Bloombox Films is excited by distinctive, fearless voices that connect with audiences globally.

    Established in 2020 to develop and produce films, TV and documentaries, Bloombox Films has a commitment to creative collaboration and champions new voices and opportunities for creators, makers and audiences.

    Bloombox Films respectfully acknowledges the Wurundjeri Woi Wurrung and Bunurong Boon Wurrung peoples of the Eastern Kulin, the Traditional Owners of the land where we work, live and learn.
